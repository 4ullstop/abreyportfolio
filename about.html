About Me

Born and raised onthe Eastern Shore of Maryland, I spent my early years fascinated with the communities that flourish from games. Whether it was by watching YouTube walk-throughs or running over to a friend's house to play the latest game that we were captivated by.

I later became well versed and excited in the logic of coding language throughout middle and high school. Knowing that these wtwo fascinatiosn could be combined led me to the decision to pursue it as a career.

Outside of games, music became a very important aspect of my life as well, earning multiple high achievements in the various solo and ensemble settings throughout high school. I also take an interest in other hobbies such as biking and 3D art modeling.
