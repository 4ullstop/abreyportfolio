Project Submarine

  • Platform: PC
  • Software: Unreal Engine
  • Creation Length: 1 year
  • Playtime: 10-15 minutes
  • Regular updates on LinkedIn

Project Submarine (Working Title) is a first-person, 2-4 player multiplayer game that promotes the best of team collaboration and chaos through intense AI encounters. Traverse through an underground facitilty controlled by a raging AI. Avoid roaming robots, traps and other inconveniences. Destroy all that is on your task list and escape!

Responsibilities

  • Develop C++ code for:
    • Creating and destroying multiplayer sessions using Unreal Engine and Steam's session interface.
    • Replicating:
      • Interaction.
      • Picking/Placing objects.
      • Using objects.
      • Death/spectator class.
      • Assigning tasks to all players.
      • Task/Mini-games.
      • Inconveniences caused by the Master AI.
      • Player animations.
  • Designing:
    • Gameplay loop.
    • Tasks.
    • AI inconveniences.
